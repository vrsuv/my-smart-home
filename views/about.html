Welcome to MySmartHome!

I, V Rajasuvedha, have developed MySmartHome for you to manage your smart devices!

You may start by adding new devices of your own by clicking the 'Add a device' link.

Or you may choose from a list of devices we have provided in our catalog to be added into your list of devices!

You can update any of the devices in the 'Update device' link.

You may also delete any of your devices in the 'Delete devices' link.

You can then view the devices in the 'View all devices' link!

Have fun managing your smart devices for a smart lifestyle!
